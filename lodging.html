Hotels

Taniti Island

Our famous four-star resort

Relax in true heavenly hospitality with the luxurious rooms of Taniti Islan Resort. No other island accommodations give you access to diverse cultural experiences and award-winning hospitality. For a truly memorable hotel experience, take advantage of our in-room amenities to enhance your getaway.


Family-owned hotels

There are many small, family-owned hotels on the Taniti Island. These hotels provide more of the local flavor while also supporting the local economy. Each hotel is a unique experience in and of itself. Reasonable pricing will allow you to experience more of what the island has to offer.


Bed and Breakfast

You will wake each morning with the fresh smell of fresh ground coffee being brewed and breakfast being prepared. Breakfast is a culinary and creative dining experience and guest will be delighted by unique flavors and dishes of Taniti cuisine.
